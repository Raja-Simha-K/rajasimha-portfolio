Hello, I'm Raja Simha K

A self-made UI/UX designer, I approach design as an art form. My journey began in the realm of film editing, where my passion for creative visuals first took root. Over time, I honed my skills and transitioned into the dynamic world of UI/UX design using youtube as a great resource.

FROM FILMMAKER TO UI/UX DESIGNER

I started as a filmmaker with a passion for storytelling and a knack for design. My journey began with a fascination for VFX in films, leading me to explore filmmaking, editing, and poster design. Along the way, I delved into Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, and After Effects to bring my ideas to life.

While pursuing my UG as Engineering, I realized my heart was in design. I started freelancing in Image retouching in 2020 and eventually landed my first official job as a UI/UX designer in 2022. Since then, I've been blending my technical skills with creative instincts to craft intuitive digital experiences.

To showcase my designs, I've dived into web development, mastering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap. I'm constantly learning and staying updated with design trends to deliver impactful solutions.

Eventually Improved my skills on UX design. I have learned to Empathize with the user, and Define the problem statement, and, Ideate the possible solution.
