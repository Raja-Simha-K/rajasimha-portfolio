Problems

Securenext needs to revamp their old website with modern design trends. They want to showcase their experience and build trust with client through the website.

We just understood that the old website has no user flow. User find hard to understand the services provided by securenext. We had the opportunity to well establish the services they provide with the good portfolios.

Research

We conducted the stakeholder interview along with my team to deeply understand Tech services they provide and hiring models they could provide.

Service

User could not find the services and could not find the tech and industry they need.

Price

User need flexible hiring models also the flexible payment methods.

Trust

User need know more about the company. They could not find the previous works and portfolios.

Brand

This website lacks the good aesthetics and brand guides. so they can stand out than other competitors.

Solution

I have collected the competitors website and conducted neccesary research and brainstormed with our team to come up with multiple Solutions to resolve or improve the issue.

Service

User who crawl the website nned the proper services page and CTA to contact. And well deisgned contact form to contact us.

Price

As securenext already has the flexible hiring module, we have analysed it and divided into three parts and added to website.

Trust

User need to examine the previous works to build trust, so we build portfolio page and also well designed testimonial.

Brand

We created the brand guide with the existing logo. And build the website bound to the guides.

Design Sprint

We conducted the stakeholder interview to understood the goals. As we already have enough details on old website, we just analysed the competitors website strategy and design aesthetics.

Industry Analysis

We analysed the current market in IT solution industry. We also analysed fortune 500 companies like TCS, Accenture, Infosys. We came up with some valuable feature that can be implemented.

Services Categorized Based on Industries

Experience our expertise on certain on Industry can attract more clients. It also improved the trust to the client and increases the chance of rentention rate.

Popular Blog and Insights

We planned to upload the insightful trending blog related to the services we provide and add CTA through blog. While client crawls for the solution our blog can make our site to list on top in google search.

Easily Accesible Navigation Menu

First 5 seconds rule - We have categorized the services and tools under common terms. For example - Flutter is the framework tool, we have categorized it under the term mobile application. Which reduces the cognitive load

Valuable CTA for Retention

The number of leads turn to customer only through the CTA. The most relatable and connectful the CTA then user retention will be improved.
